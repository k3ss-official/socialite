## 1. SNAPSHOT

Scran Away is a sandwich shop / breakfast café at 73 Bolton Street, Chorley PR7 3AG, trading since ~early 2025, with a 5 (Very Good) hygiene rating from Chorley Borough Council (inspected 25 Mar 2025). The product is clearly landing: 100% recommend across 68 Facebook reviews, a perfect 5.0 on Google, and review language like "this is my new spot" and "cheaper than anywhere near." Facebook is actively run (posting within the last day, warm owner voice). **The single strongest reason they'll say yes:** they have a 5.0-rated business that Google can barely see — 20 Google reviews and no listed hours, while every competitor in town has 113–1,114 reviews. The demand is proven; the visibility is missing; we bolt the visibility on.
